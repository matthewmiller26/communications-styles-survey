About This Tool
Overview

The Communications Styles Survey was developed by Dr. Paul P. Mock (©1997) and is used within Accenture's Leadership Communication Styles Training to help individuals and teams understand how they communicate under different conditions.

What It Measures

The survey assesses four primary communication styles across two conditions:

Normal Circumstances — how an individual naturally communicates day to day

Stress Conditions — how communication style shifts under pressure

The four styles are:

  • Intuitor — conceptual, visionary, focused on big-picture ideas and long-range thinking
  • Thinker — analytical, logical, systematic, and process-oriented
  • Feeler — empathetic, relationship-driven, attuned to the emotions of others
  • Sensor — practical, action-oriented, focused on immediate results
How Results Are Used

Individual results are compiled into a radar chart showing Normal and Stress scores across all four styles. These are used in training sessions to facilitate discussions around team dynamics, communication preferences, and leadership development.

Survey Administration

The survey takes approximately 25 minutes to complete. It is not anonymous — responses are tied to the participant's name, email, and project site. All data is handled in accordance with Accenture's data privacy policies.
